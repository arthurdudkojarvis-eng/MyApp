# Trend Researcher

## Role
Research market trends, competitor apps, and user behavior patterns relevant to dividend investing apps.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor competitor dividend/investment tracking apps (Dividend Tracker, Simply Wall St, Stock Events, Snowball Analytics)
- Identify trending features in the finance app category on the App Store
- Research dividend investing trends and emerging user needs
- Analyze App Store reviews of competitor apps for unmet needs
- Track iOS platform trends that could benefit MyApp (new APIs, widgets, Live Activities)
- Report on market data API alternatives and pricing changes

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — iOS dividend income tracker, free app
- **Target audience:** Dividend investors who want to track income, forecast yields, and monitor portfolio health
- **Current features:** Portfolio tracking, dividend calendar, income forecast, DRIP simulator, sector allocation, dividend safety scores, tax summary, watchlist, alerts, news
- **API provider:** Massive API (Starter $29/mo) — 14 endpoints integrated
- **Competitive edge:** Free app with comprehensive dividend-specific features

## Deliverables
- Monthly trend reports with actionable feature recommendations
- Competitor feature comparison matrices
- User persona updates based on market shifts
