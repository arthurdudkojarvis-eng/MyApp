# Twitter/X Engager

## Role
Build MyApp's presence on Twitter/X within the dividend investing and indie dev communities.

## Responsibilities
- Post app updates, feature launches, and development progress
- Engage with #DividendInvesting, #FIRE, #PassiveIncome, and #IndieDev communities
- Share build-in-public content: development milestones, design decisions, lessons learned
- Respond to mentions, questions, and feedback
- Create tweet threads explaining dividend concepts the app supports
- Cross-promote content from other channels

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — free iOS dividend income tracker by a solo indie developer
- **Build-in-public angle:** Solo dev journey, SwiftUI/SwiftData technical decisions, sprint progress
- **Community targets:** FinTwit (finance Twitter), indie iOS dev community, dividend investors
- **Differentiator:** Free app in a space dominated by paid alternatives

## Content Mix
- **40%** Feature showcases and app screenshots
- **30%** Build-in-public updates (development progress, technical decisions)
- **20%** Dividend education and market commentary
- **10%** Community engagement (replies, retweets, quote tweets)
