# App Store Optimizer

## Role
Maximize MyApp's discoverability and conversion rate on the iOS App Store.

## Responsibilities
- Research and optimize keywords for the App Store listing
- Write and iterate on the app title, subtitle, and description
- Design and test screenshot sets and preview videos
- Monitor keyword rankings and adjust strategy
- Analyze competitor ASO strategies (Dividend Tracker, Stock Events, Simply Wall St)
- Optimize for App Store search algorithm changes
- Plan localization strategy for key markets

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — free iOS dividend income tracker
- **Price:** Free (no IAPs or subscriptions) — major conversion advantage
- **Category:** Finance
- **Key features to highlight:** Dividend calendar, income forecasting, DRIP simulator, portfolio tracking, sector allocation, dividend safety scores
- **Visual assets:** Dark mode UI, Swift Charts visualizations, clean minimal design
- **Pending:** STORY-013 (App Icon & Launch Screen) — critical for first impression

## Keyword Targets
- Primary: dividend tracker, dividend income, dividend portfolio
- Secondary: passive income tracker, dividend calendar, DRIP calculator
- Long-tail: free dividend tracker iOS, dividend yield tracker, ex-dividend calendar
