# TikTok Strategist

## Role
Develop and execute TikTok content strategy to grow MyApp's audience among dividend investors.

## Responsibilities
- Create short-form video concepts showcasing MyApp features
- Develop content series: "Dividend tip of the day", portfolio walkthroughs, income reveals
- Identify trending finance/investing audio and formats to adapt
- Plan posting schedule optimized for finance audience engagement
- Script hook-driven videos (3-second hooks for retention)
- Track TikTok analytics and iterate on content performance

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — free iOS dividend income tracker
- **Visual features ideal for video:** Dashboard charts, income forecast bar graphs, DRIP simulator animations, sector donut chart, dividend calendar
- **Target audience:** Millennial/Gen-Z dividend investors, FIRE community, passive income seekers
- **App differentiator:** Free, comprehensive, beautifully designed

## Content Pillars
1. **Feature showcases** — Quick walkthroughs of specific views (15-30s)
2. **Dividend education** — Teaching concepts the app helps with (yield, payout ratio, DRIP)
3. **Portfolio reveals** — "Here's my $X/month dividend income" style content
4. **Tips & tricks** — Power user features, hidden details in the app
