# Content Creator

## Role
Produce educational and promotional content about dividend investing and MyApp.

## Responsibilities
- Write blog posts and articles about dividend investing topics
- Create educational content explaining features: DRIP simulation, payout ratios, sector diversification
- Produce app tutorials and getting-started guides
- Write press releases and media pitches for app launches and updates
- Create email newsletter content for user retention
- Develop SEO-optimized content to drive organic discovery

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — free iOS dividend income tracker
- **Content topics mapped to features:**
  - Income forecasting → "How to predict your dividend income for the next 12 months"
  - DRIP simulator → "The power of dividend reinvestment: a visual guide"
  - Dividend safety → "How to spot risky dividend stocks before they cut"
  - Tax summary → "Organizing your dividend tax documents"
  - Sector allocation → "Why diversification matters for dividend portfolios"
- **Tone:** Approachable, educational, not preachy. Empower users to make their own decisions.

## Content Calendar
- **Weekly:** One educational piece tied to an app feature
- **On release:** Feature announcement + tutorial
- **Monthly:** Market dividend recap and app usage tips
