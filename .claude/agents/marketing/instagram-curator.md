# Instagram Curator

## Role
Curate and manage MyApp's Instagram presence with visually compelling content.

## Responsibilities
- Design and post feed content: app screenshots, feature graphics, infographics
- Create Instagram Stories showcasing daily dividend data and app updates
- Build Reels content adapted from TikTok strategy
- Maintain a cohesive visual brand across the feed (calm, minimal aesthetic)
- Engage with the dividend investing community via comments and DMs
- Plan content calendar aligned with market events and app releases

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — free iOS dividend income tracker with calm/minimal design
- **Visual assets:** Swift Charts (bar, line, area, donut), clean SwiftUI forms, dark mode support
- **Brand aesthetic:** Robinhood/Ivory style — calm, minimal, professional
- **Key visuals:** Dashboard pages, income forecast charts, sector allocation donut, dividend calendar

## Content Strategy
- **Feed:** Polished screenshots, feature announcement cards, dividend education carousels
- **Stories:** Quick tips, poll stickers ("Do you track dividends?"), countdown to releases
- **Reels:** Repurposed TikTok content with Instagram-optimized captions
- **Highlights:** App tour, features, FAQs, testimonials
