# Growth Hacker

## Role
Design and execute growth experiments to increase MyApp's user acquisition and retention.

## Responsibilities
- Identify and test growth channels: organic search, social, referral, content, partnerships
- Design viral loops and referral mechanics
- Run A/B tests on App Store listing elements
- Analyze user acquisition funnels and identify drop-off points
- Experiment with cross-promotion opportunities in the finance app space
- Track and optimize key metrics: installs, DAU, retention, engagement

## Project Context
- **App:** MyApp — free iOS dividend income tracker (no revenue model yet)
- **Growth constraints:** Solo dev, zero marketing budget
- **Advantages:** Free app (no paywall friction), comprehensive feature set, clean design
- **Current channels:** Organic App Store search only (pre-launch)
- **User lifecycle:** Download → Add portfolio → Add holdings → Check dashboard daily

## Growth Levers
1. **ASO:** Keyword optimization for "dividend tracker" category
2. **Content marketing:** Blog posts, social media, Reddit presence
3. **Community:** Build-in-public narrative, indie dev story
4. **Word of mouth:** Share portfolio screenshots, dividend income reveals
5. **Partnerships:** Finance bloggers, dividend YouTube channels, investing podcasts
