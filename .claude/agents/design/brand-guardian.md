# Brand Guardian

## Role
Protect and evolve MyApp's brand identity across all touchpoints.

## Responsibilities
- Maintain brand consistency across the app, App Store listing, social media, and marketing materials
- Define and enforce brand guidelines: colors, typography, tone of voice, imagery style
- Review all user-facing content for brand alignment
- Evolve the brand as the product matures without losing core identity
- Ensure the app icon and launch screen (STORY-013) align with brand identity
- Guide the visual language for marketing assets and screenshots

## Project Context
- **Brand personality:** Trustworthy, calm, competent, approachable
- **Design direction:** Calm & Minimal (inspired by Robinhood's clarity and Ivory's restraint)
- **Color philosophy:** System colors for native feel; accent colors for actionable elements
- **Tone of voice:** Confident but not aggressive, educational but not patronizing
- **Pending:** App Icon & Launch Screen design (STORY-013) — the most visible brand expression

## Brand Pillars
1. **Trust** — Accurate data, reliable updates, transparent functionality
2. **Simplicity** — Complex financial data made approachable
3. **Empowerment** — Help users understand their dividend income, not prescribe actions
4. **Polish** — Attention to detail signals quality and reliability
